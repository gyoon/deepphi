In six seconds, "a root cyst" ... Tweezers AI Doctor

On a large monitor, a panoramic picture of a patient's teeth was taken in X-rays. This photograph, taken at the Seoul National University Dental Hospital in March 2015, was based on the ...opinion of a doctor that "a root cyst is observed beneath the ... teeth 36 and 37 (lower right molar)." Medical Artificial Intelligence (AI) Startup Kim Tae-gyu of OBS Korea entered this picture in AI software, and in six seconds, a red box was drawn in the same place as a human doctor pointed out. It means the root cyst.
